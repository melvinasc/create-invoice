# 🧾 Create Invoice — Fast, Modern Invoice Generator

**Create Invoice** is a lightweight, browser-based invoicing tool designed for freelancers, small businesses, and agencies who need to generate professional invoices in seconds — without complex software or subscriptions.

👉 **Live App:** https://melvinasc.github.io/create-invoice/

---

## 🚀 Why Create Invoice?

Traditional invoicing tools are often:
- Too slow for quick jobs
- Locked behind subscriptions
- Overcomplicated for simple billing needs

**Create Invoice solves this by focusing on speed, simplicity, and control.**

You open it → fill in details → export a professional invoice → done.

No accounts. No setup. No friction.

## ✨ Key Features

### ⚡ Instant Invoice Creation
Generate professional invoices directly in your browser in under a minute.

### 🧮 Smart Auto Calculations
Automatically calculates:
- Subtotal  
- Taxes  
- Discounts (if enabled)  
- Final total  

### 📄 Export Ready (PDF)
Download clean, print-ready invoices instantly.

### 🏢 Fully Customizable
- Company details  
- Client information  
- Line items  
- Currency & tax settings  

### 📱 Responsive Design
Works seamlessly across:
- Desktop
- Tablet
- Mobile

### 🔒 Privacy First
Everything runs locally in your browser.
No data is stored. No tracking. No sign-up.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. Enter your business & client details  
2. Add invoice items  
3. Adjust tax or discounts  
4. Preview invoice in real time  
5. Export as PDF  

That’s it.
